**Tamil Nadu Water Supply and Drainage Board** (**TWAD Board**) is a public agency formed by the [Government of Tamil Nadu](/wiki/Government_of_Tamil_Nadu "Government of Tamil Nadu" ), assigned with task of providing [water supply](/wiki/Water_supply "Water supply" ) and [sewerage](/wiki/Sanitary_sewer "Sanitary sewer" ) facilities to the state of [Tamil Nadu](/wiki/Tamil_Nadu "Tamil Nadu" ) (except the [Chennai Metropolitan Area](/wiki/Chennai_Metropolitan_Area "Chennai Metropolitan Area" )). It was formed on April 14, 1971.[1]
